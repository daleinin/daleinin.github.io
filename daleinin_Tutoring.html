  • Experience: (proffessional tutorial centers, not inlcuding private tutoring)

    • Jan. 2016 - Present
      • Math Multimedia Center (MMC): is a free drop-in tutorial center run and operated by the Math Department at North Carolina State University where math grad students are made available to help students on material related to any math courses and is in general a fantastic resource for students. As a grad student, I have been working in the MMC every Fall and Spring semester since the Spring of 2015-2016 academic year. In addition, I worked in the MMC part-time during first summer session of 2017.

    • Aug. 2015 - Jan. 2016
      • TRIO Programs: is a collection of programs focused on providing additional academic resources and support to help ensure that every student has the opportunity to move from high school to college and beyond. I was employed by the Student Support Services (SSS) branch of the program where I worked as a one-on-one mathematics tutor primarly helping students with calculus one, two, three, and ordinary differential equations.

    • Aug. 2014 - Dec. 2014
      • Physics Tutorial Center (PTC): is a drop-in tutorial center run and operated by the Physics Department at North Carolina State University. While similar in style to the MMC, the PTC also often hires advanced undergraduates as well as graduate students in the physics department. While employed in the PTC, we were free to help any student on any physics related material.
